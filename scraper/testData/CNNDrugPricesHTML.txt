 <h2>The U.S. Senate has launched an investigation into Turing Pharmaceuticals -- the drug company that hiked the price of a life-saving drug by 5,000%.</h2> <p> In a <a href='http://www.aging.senate.gov/imo/media/doc/TuringDocumentRequest.pdf' target='_blank'>letter</a><strong> </strong>released<strong> </strong>Wednesday, the Senate Special Committee on Aging said it was looking into the "pricing of off-patent drugs," and requested information from Turing CEO Martin Shkreli. </p> <div id="ie_column"> <div id="ie_column"> <div id="quigo220"><!-- ADSPACE: technology/quigo/ctr.220x200 -->
	<div id="ad_ns_btf_03"></div>
<!-- ADSPACE-END --></div> </div> </div> <p> The committee will investigate Turing's "acquisition of the rights to sell Daraprim," and the decision to raise the price from $13.50 a pill to $750.00. </p> <p> Daraprim is a drug used by some AIDS and cancer patients to treat a life-threatening, parasitic infection called toxoplasmosis. </p> <p> Although Daraprim has been around for decades, Shkreli's Turing Pharmaceuticals only acquired the rights to sell the drug in August. Soon after, Turing hiked the price. </p> <p> <a href='/2015/10/23/media/martin-shkreli-cnbc-war/index.html' target='_blank'><span class='inStoryHeading'>Related: Martin Shkreli goes to war with CNBC</span></a> </p> <p> Despite publicly saying he would <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2015/09/22/investing/daraprim-aids-drug-price/index.html?iid=EL'>lower the cost</a>, the drug remains <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2015/10/09/investing/drug-ceo-daraprim-price/?iid=EL'>prohibitively expensive.</a> </p> <p> Shkreli has been ordered to provide a variety of<strong> </strong>documentation and information about Turing's business to the committee by December 2, 2015. </p> <p> Among the committee's requests, it asked for the identity of "the Turing employee responsible for setting the price of Daraprim." </p> <p> The drug pricing controversy even sparked reaction from Democratic presidential candidates. </p> <p> <a href='/2015/10/20/news/companies/hillary-clinton-drug-prices-daraprim/index.html' target='_blank'>Hillary Clinton</a> called for an investigation into Turing and criticized drug companies for price gouging. </p> <p> <a href='/2015/10/16/news/companies/sanders-drug-ceo-shkreli-donation/index.html' target='_blank'>Bernie Sanders</a> rejected a $2,700 campaign donation from Shkreli. Shkreli didn't take the rejection well -- he said he was so angry at Sanders <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2015/10/18/news/companies/turing-ceo-martin-shkreli-bernie-sanders/index.html'>he "could punch a wall!"</a> </p> <p> <a href='/2015/10/20/investing/drug-price-hikes-martin-shkreli-valeant/index.html' target='_blank'><span class='inStoryHeading'>Related: Sticker shock: Drugs with price hikes of up to 1,200%</span></a> </p> <div id="storyFooter"></div> <div class="clearfix"></div> <div class="storytimestamp"> <span class="cnnStorySource"> CNNMoney (New York) </span> <span class="cnnDateStamp">November 5, 2015: 10:45 AM ET</span> </div> </div> <!--/storytext--> <div class="foot"> <div id="postedin"></div> <div class="clearFloat"></div> </div> </div> <!-- main --> <div class="share-tools pull-left" id="js-sharebar-main"></div>
